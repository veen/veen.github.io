Grandma Slaymaker's Story

Leslie always brought her friends home for the family to meet and then they were always welcome to come to my home and help celebrate holidays and special occasions. We liked her choice of friends and thought she would get serious about one in particular but then she brought a tall, lanky fellow home for the weekend and I wondered what she could possibly see in him. I inquired about his education and what his plans for the future would be and he told me he was going to take another year of college. I thought this was ridiculous, get out and get a job and start earning money. Now I can see that there is something guiding him in a specific direction.
