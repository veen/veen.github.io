Joanne and David's Story

When you think about Manhattan, what comes to mind? Probably the theater, the art museums, the subway. Maybe shopping at Bloomingdales, dinner at the Russian Tea Room, tea at the Waldorf, or pastrami at the Carnegie Deli. Maybe landmarks, like the United Nations, the Empire State Building, the Statue of Liberty.

When you think about Manhattan, you don't usually think about rugged, outdoor activities. You don't usually think about...

...kayaking.

Jeff and Leslie are the only couple I know who would go to Manhattan, and go kayaking.
