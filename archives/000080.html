Name that war

25 February 2003

What will we call the upcoming senseless war in Iraq? Here are some good suggestions, taken from a recent column in the Oregonian forwarded to me by my friend Victor.

Operation Iraqnophobia.
Operation Reelection.
Operation Infinite Oil.
Operation Oil's Well That Ends Well.
The Blair-Bush Project.
Operation Saddamectomy.
Operation Deficit Storm.
Operation I Want a War Just Like the War Started (but Inexplicably Not Finished) by Dear Ol' Dad.
Curious George Kills a Bunch of People and Alienates All His Friends.
Operation Save My SUV.
Operation Daddy Says Knock You Out.
The Operation Formerly Known as Desert Storm.
