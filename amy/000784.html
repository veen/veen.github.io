Almost home...

September 09, 2005

We just came back from the Mommy / Baby Camp class and got yet another round of good information. The great thing about the way they work here at the hospital is that they assume you're not going to absorb anything they're saying to you (which we didn't), so they say it again and again. It's funny having that ah-ha moment when what they're saying actually clicks and the day-to-day tasks begin to actually make some sense.

Hopefully we'll remember this stuff at 3AM changings and feedings. :)

Sophia's got one more set of tests, then we eat lunch and then we're out of here!!! Time to bring our little girl home to her room (and Daddy's office) and see whether or not she likes life with the Fraziers. Especially Sarah the Dog. I sure hope Sophie doesn't mind doggie kisses when we let Sarah come see her in a few weeks!

BTW - Thanks to everyone for your encouragement and prayers. We feel like God has taken care of us through this whole process and that he listens to the prayers of the faithful. Blessings to all of you!
