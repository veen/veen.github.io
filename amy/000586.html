Bowl Becomes Eclectic

July 26, 2004

Many times I gripe about the woes of living in LA, but then I have a night like last night, and I remember why I love it here. We went to World Electronica at the Hollywood Bowl last night. It’s basically a global electronica experience at LA’s coolest outdoor venue. It was the perfect summer night – sitting under the stars grooving to The Crystal Method and Nortec Collective. And even better - it’s sponsored by KCRW, my absolute favorite radio station. Its listener supported and plays all manner of eclectic and new music, and also broadcasts NPR. Go listen live now!
