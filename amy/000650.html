Coolest. Artist. Ever.

November 09, 2004

One of my newest favorites in the realm of art is Tim Biskup. Check out his website for a sample of his work. Or, better yet - if you're in the Pasadena area, see it for yourself in person at his newly opened Bispop Gallery inside Johnson Motors Store in Old Town. It's really fun and really ecclectic.

And the T-shirts totally rock.
