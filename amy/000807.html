Good news is so relative

October 27, 2005

So I called up my mom today, totally excited about the fact that Sophie only woke up once during the night for the first time.

"I've got great news!" I tell her, ready to brag about my 7-week-old's 8pm-3am stretch of sleep.

"You're pregnant!?" she screams.

In whose world would that be good news? Anyway, sorry for the letdown, Grandma.
