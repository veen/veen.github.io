Veterans: we honor you

November 11, 2004

From my Uncle Bob, who is himself a very cool veteran:

"Armistice Day, Armistice Day, That's All I Really Wanted To Say" ...Paul Simon

"This time of year I can't help but put on my old fatigue jacket and think of those with whom I served; especially those friends, acquaintances, commanders, pilots, and just fellow GI's who were denied the luxury to see life in the 21st century.

Doesn't matter who is in charge, those who answer the call will be there for America. It is America who must ensure the call is just, honest, and lives lost not in vain."

--Bob Tolsma

Or, better yet - if you're in the Pasadena area, see it for yourself in person at his newly opened Bispop Gallery inside Johnson Motors Store in Old Town. It's really fun and really ecclectic.

And the T-shirts totally rock.
