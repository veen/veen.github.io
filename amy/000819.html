Sleep rules!

November 19, 2005

The illustrious day has finally arrived. And much sooner than I thought it would, actually. My wonderful baby is sleeping through the night! We're talking good old-fashinoned, down home 7pm-7am sleep. The first night this happened, earlier this week, I wasn't so sure. I was convinced it was a fluke and if I mentioned it to anyone the lords of fate would jinx me and my sleep-filled nights would disappear. But then it happened the next night. And again. And 4 nights later, I think it's safe to say that my baby likes to sleep. Like really likes to sleep.

And I am forever grateful!
