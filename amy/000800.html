Pumpkins!

October 12, 2005


Sleepy Bee
Originally uploaded by amyfrazier.
This weekend we did what all new parents do. We dressed up our poor one-month-old daughter in a ridiculous (and cute!) costume and showed her off. We actually went to an amazing Pumpkin Patch in Orange County with some friends and their kids (also dressed up, of course).

Sophie tried out her Halloween costume for a few shots. But nothing would get her to open her eyes - not even the acres and acres of pumpkins surrounding us!

It was definitely one of those events that you realize is much more for the parents' enjoyment. I'm sure there will be plenty of times in the future when I'll be doing things purely for my daughter's delight, so I figured she could humor me just this once!
