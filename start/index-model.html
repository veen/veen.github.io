Start!, a Conference for Entrepreneurs

Quit your day job!

Start is a one-day conference in San Francisco designed for smart, talented Web people to take hold of their ideas, follow their dreams, and start their own companies.

We’ve lined up entrepreneurs from some of your favorite startups as well as some interesting investors, lawyers, and others. They'll give you practical advice, horror stories, and inspiration based on years of experience. If you've ever dreamt of taking the plunge, don't miss this chance to hear from the experts!

What you’ll learn

  • Get inspired. Hear how they got started, how they screwed up, and how they kept going.
  • Hiring? Firing?!? Who’s watching the books? Do we need a lawyer?
  • Pitch off! Listen to some of the web’s best entrepreneurs give their best 5 minute pitch. Learn how to hone yours.

The day’s events

  • Jeff and Bryan show up hammered. Jeff walks away from cab leaving Bryan to pay.
  • Jeff mumbles something about web standards while Bryan takes a recruitment call from Obama ’08.
  • After a few panels and whatnot Bryan announces that there’s a bar nearby. Jeff is already there.
