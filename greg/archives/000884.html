Forward, Russia

2 May 2006

I've been looking for a band with a very specific sound, and I think I've found it in Forward, Russia: they're what it would sound like if you crammed early-stage U2 into a tiny cage and repeatedly jabbed them with a stick until they could no longer contain their rage. Just listen to their track "Fifteen, Part 2". Wow! Rock and roll: I love you.
