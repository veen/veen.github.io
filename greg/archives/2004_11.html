Stick figures in peril

5 November 2004

Stick Figures in Peril, a Flickr group I created a few weeks ago to celebrate photos of warning signs showing stick figures in dangerous situations, has been getting some great contributions lately. And now that FlickrBlog has mentioned it, I'm hoping to see even more great shots of helpless humanoids in life threatening encounters with our increasingly dangerous surroundings. Check it out, and, for crying out loud, submit some photos!

And thanks to the clever folks at Flickr, you can use a Stick Figures in Peril XML Feed (RSS 2.0 or Atom) to see submissions as they're added.
